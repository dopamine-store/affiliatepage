Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

Why did I receive this?

You've been selected as a campus ambassador or fest coordinator for The Dopamine Store! We partner with premier institutions (IIT Bombay, IIT Hyderabad, IISER Trivandrum, and others) and work exclusively with their official representatives. This page explains how the program works.

Who can become a campus ambassador?

This program is exclusively for campus ambassadors, fest coordinators, and official college representatives from our partner institutions (IIT Bombay, IIT Hyderabad, IISER Trivandrum, and others). It's not open to the general public.

How is the commission tracked?

Each ambassador receives a unique coupon code. Our system automatically tracks every time your code is used for a purchase, and attributes that sale to you for commission calculation.

When and how do I receive commission reports?

We send periodic email reports showing the number of times your coupon code was used and your total commission earned. The frequency depends on the agreement with your institution.

What products can I promote?

You can promote our entire product catalog including anime merchandise, oversized t-shirts, classic tees, premium mousepads, and college-specific collections. Check out thedopaminestore.in for the full range.

Is there a minimum number of sales required?

No! There's no minimum sales target. You earn commission from your very first sale, and there's no upper limit either.

How do I get started?

You're already set up! You have your unique coupon code. Simply start sharing our product links along with your code to your college community via WhatsApp groups, social media, or in person. Every time someone makes a purchase using your code, you earn 10% commission.
